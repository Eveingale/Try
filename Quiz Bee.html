Proper Grammar

Grammar is the set of rules that governs the structure of sentences. It includes the correct use of words, phrases, and punctuation. Here are some basic grammar rules:

  • Use capital letters at the beginning of sentences.
  • Always use a period, exclamation mark, or question mark at the end of a sentence.
  • Use commas to separate items in a list.
  • Use apostrophes to show possession or to form contractions.

Vocabulary

Vocabulary refers to the set of words that are familiar to a person. Here are some tips for improving your vocabulary:

  • Read regularly to encounter new words.
  • Use a dictionary or thesaurus to understand and find synonyms for words.
  • Practice using new words in sentences.
